Onboarding Checklist

Facility Onboarding & Contracting Checklist

Use this before go-live so contracting, scheduling, billing, privacy, and day-of-service workflow are settled in one place.

1. Contract Setup

  • [ ] Legal facility name confirmed
  • [ ] Authorized signer identified
  • [ ] Rate sheet approved
  • [ ] Net terms, late fees, travel radius, and cancellation terms confirmed
  • [ ] Report turnaround expectations written into the agreement

2. Billing Workflow

  • [ ] Accounts payable contact identified
  • [ ] Primary payer lanes reviewed: Part A, Part B, MA, Medicaid, private pay
  • [ ] Internal owner assigned for denials, audits, and claim questions
  • [ ] Contract language states reimbursement is never guaranteed
  • [ ] Process defined for residents who may need patient-pay approval before service

3. Privacy & Access

  • [ ] Workflow reviewed to decide whether a BAA is required
  • [ ] Secure method chosen for receiving referrals and sending reports
  • [ ] Facility contact knows how images or clips will be handled
  • [ ] Records-retention expectations aligned with facility policy

4. Referral Intake Workflow

  • [ ] Order or referral source defined
  • [ ] Facility uses a standard packet for demographics, diet, diagnosis, and reason for study
  • [ ] Day-of-service nursing/therapy contact identified
  • [ ] Preferred scheduling windows documented
  • [ ] Expectations for family attendance or care-team review documented

5. Launch Readiness

  • [ ] First referral date tentatively planned
  • [ ] Staff know when to use FEES versus outpatient MBSS
  • [ ] Facility packet shared with billing, rehab, and leadership
  • [ ] Escalation plan established for urgent or same-day requests
This checklist is an operational planning tool, not legal advice. Final contract language should be reviewed by the facility's legal and compliance team.
