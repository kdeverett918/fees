Facility Referral Checklist

Use this before sending a resident for mobile FEES or bedside swallowing consultation.

Resident and Billing Information

  • [ ] Resident full name and date of birth
  • [ ] Medical record number
  • [ ] Current unit / room number
  • [ ] Payer status confirmed: Part A, Part B, MA, Medicaid, private pay
  • [ ] Facility billing contact identified

Clinical Packet

  • [ ] Physician or qualified-practitioner order obtained if required
  • [ ] Primary diagnosis and relevant comorbidities included
  • [ ] Current diet and liquid consistency listed
  • [ ] Reason instrumental assessment is needed now
  • [ ] Recent chest imaging, pneumonia history, or aspiration concerns if relevant
  • [ ] Current medications and major precautions

Day-of-Service Preparation

  • [ ] Primary facility contact confirmed
  • [ ] Resident available at the scheduled time
  • [ ] Preferred snacks, liquids, or usual meal items available when appropriate
  • [ ] Family or caregiver notified if they should attend
  • [ ] Nursing and therapy team know where recommendations will be delivered

After the Study

  • [ ] Facility reviews same-day verbal recommendations
  • [ ] Written report routed to the chart and provider
  • [ ] Orders, diet, and care plan updated as needed
  • [ ] Billing pathway documented internally
Stronger referrals usually mean fewer delays, fewer reschedules, and a cleaner billing pathway after the study.
