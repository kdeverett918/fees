CMS explains that uninsured or self-pay individuals are entitled to a Good Faith Estimate of expected charges for scheduled care or on request. The estimate should reflect the services reasonably expected to be provided, but it is not a guarantee of final charges.
This worksheet is used to prepare the estimate. The actual Good Faith Estimate should be issued separately and reviewed with the patient before service.
