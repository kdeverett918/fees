This Facility Services Agreement is entered into by and between [Facility legal name] ("Facility") and Mobile FEES LV / Kristine Everett, MA, CCC-SLP ("Provider") effective [date].

1. Scope of Services

Provider will furnish mobile Fiberoptic Endoscopic Evaluation of Swallowing (FEES), clinical swallowing evaluations, dysphagia consultation, and related education services at Facility for residents referred by Facility and approved by Provider for scheduling.

2. Orders and Referral Packet

  • Facility will obtain the physician or qualified-practitioner order required by its workflow or payer.
  • Facility will provide demographics, diagnoses, current diet, payer status, and reason instrumental assessment is needed.
  • Provider may refuse or reschedule a referral if required documentation is incomplete.

4. Payment Terms

  • Facility will pay Provider within [Net 15 / Net 30] days of invoice.
  • Late payments may accrue a finance charge of [1.5%] per month.
  • Facility remains financially responsible for Provider's invoice regardless of payer outcome unless a separate written amendment states otherwise.

5. Insurance, Consolidated Billing, and Payer Verification

Facility acknowledges that for residents in a Medicare Part A SNF stay, swallowing services arranged under this Agreement are generally handled inside Facility's consolidated billing / PPS structure rather than billed separately by Provider.

For Part B, managed-care, Medicaid, or private-pay residents, Facility will determine whether the service will be billed under Facility's own workflow, absorbed by Facility, or handled through an approved patient-pay arrangement established before service.

Provider supplies the clinical service and supporting documentation, but Provider does not guarantee reimbursement, prior authorization, or payer approval.

6. Scheduling, Cancellation, and No-Show Policy

  • Scheduling is subject to Provider availability.
  • Facility will identify a day-of-service contact.
  • A cancellation fee of [insert amount] may apply with less than 24 hours notice.
  • A late cancellation fee may also apply when the resident is unavailable or the order is not ready.

7. Documentation and Communication

  • Provider will furnish a written report to Facility and the referring team.
  • Facility is responsible for integrating recommendations into its own care plan, orders, and chart workflow.
  • Provider may supply a superbill or coding support when requested, but claim submission remains Facility's responsibility unless otherwise agreed in writing.

8. HIPAA and Business Associate Terms

The parties will comply with HIPAA and applicable state privacy laws. If required by the parties' workflow, the parties will execute a Business Associate Agreement.

9. Independent Contractor

Provider is an independent contractor and not an employee of Facility. Nothing in this Agreement creates employment, partnership, or joint venture status.

10. Term and Termination

This Agreement begins on the effective date and continues for twelve (12) months unless terminated earlier. Either party may terminate with thirty (30) days written notice, or immediately for material breach that is not cured.
