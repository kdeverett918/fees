Concierge Patient Service Agreement

Use for private-pay home visits or other concierge arrangements. Pair with the consent and financial policy form.

This agreement is between Mobile FEES LV and [patient / responsible party name] for concierge swallowing services on [date].

1. Services Included

  • Concierge FEES home visit or clinical swallow consultation, as scheduled.
  • Review of history, current diet, and referral reason.
  • Same-day discussion of findings and recommendations.
  • Written report sent to the referring medical team.

3. Payment Terms

  • Payment is due before or at the time of service unless otherwise arranged in writing.
  • Accepted payment methods: [insert method].
  • Good Faith Estimate available on request before scheduling.

4. Insurance and Superbills

Concierge services are typically private pay first. Mobile FEES LV may provide a superbill for out-of-network submission when appropriate, but payment by Medicare, Medicaid, Medicare Advantage, or commercial insurance is not guaranteed.

5. Cancellation Policy

  • Please provide at least 24 hours notice when canceling or rescheduling.
  • A cancellation fee of [insert amount] may apply for late cancellations or no-shows.
  • Unsafe home conditions or missing medical information may require rescheduling.

6. Medical Coordination

The patient or responsible party agrees to provide current medical information and to identify the referring physician or practitioner. The service does not replace emergency care or hospital-based evaluation when a higher level of medical support is needed.

Reminder: a concierge home visit may still lead to recommendation for outpatient MBSS, emergency evaluation, or physician follow-up depending on findings.
