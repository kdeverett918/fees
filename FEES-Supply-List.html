Your Cleaning Workflow (Between Every Patient)

Your ENTity XL is non-channeled — no internal tubes to brush or flush. The entire process is external wipe + soak. Total time: ~20 minutes.

1

Pre-Clean (Immediately After Use)

Wipe the insertion tube with a Metrex MetriSponge (pre-saturated enzymatic sponge). Don't let bioburden dry on the scope.

2

Leak Test

Connect your ATT-OP Optim leak tester to the scope's leak test port. Pressurize, submerge in clean water, flex the bending section. Watch for continuous bubbles = leak (stop, send for repair). No bubbles = proceed.

3

Enzymatic Wash

In a basin, dilute McKesson Multi-Enzymatic Cleanser per label instructions. Submerge scope and gently wipe all exterior surfaces with a soft cloth. Soak 1–2 minutes.

4

Rinse

Rinse scope thoroughly with clean water to remove all enzymatic detergent residue.

5

HLD Soak — MetriCide OPA Plus (12 minutes)

Check solution concentration with OPA test strip before each use session. Fully immerse scope in MetriCide OPA Plus for 12 minutes at room temperature. Use a lidded basin to contain low fumes.

6

Rinse

Rinse scope thoroughly with clean water (3 rinses recommended for OPA products).

7

Dry & Store

Dry with a lint-free cloth. Store scope in clean carrying case. Do not coil tightly.

Do NOT use: Bleach, alcohol as sole disinfectant, autoclave/steam, or ultrasonic cleaners. These will destroy the scope. Do NOT soak in OPA for more than 1 hour.

Important Notes

MetriCide OPA Plus — Key Facts

• 28-day reuse: Once you pour it into a basin, it's good for 28 days. Write the open date on the basin with a marker.
• Test before every use session: Dip an OPA test strip before each day's first soak. If it fails, replace the solution.
• OPA stains gray: Wear gloves. If it gets on skin or clothes, it will stain. If it contacts proteins (dried blood on scope), it stains permanently — that's why pre-cleaning is critical.
• Low fumes but use a lid: OPA has mild fumes. Use a lidded basin. If you're doing multiple soaks in a small room, crack a window.
• Do NOT exceed 1 hour soak time. Longer exposure can degrade scope adhesives.

Leak Tester — Key Facts

• Olympus/Pentax/Fujinon testers will NOT fit your ENTity XL. Different connector.
• Only the ATT-OP (Optim-specific adapter) works.
• Leak test port location: On the handle/proximal body of the scope. Small capped valve. Check your IFU document for exact position.
• Call Optim (800-225-7486) to ask about the port gauge if you want to source a third-party adapter.
• Call ESS (845-277-1700) to buy the ATT-OP or ask about compatibility.
