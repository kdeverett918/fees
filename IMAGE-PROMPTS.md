## Tips for Best Results

- **Aspect ratios:** Hero/banner = 16:9, Cards = 4:3, Portraits = 3:4, Icons = 1:1
- **Color palette:** Deep teal + warm amber/gold + clean whites
- **Tone:** Professional but warm, medical but not sterile, reassuring not scary
- **Equipment:** Always reference "Optim ENTity XL portable endoscopy system" when showing FEES equipment
- **Patients:** Older adults (60+), diverse representation, comfortable and calm
- **Setting:** SNF rooms, rehab facilities, assisted living — NOT hospital ORs or sterile environments
- **Your real photos are always better** — swap AI images for your own clinical photos (with patient consent) whenever possible
